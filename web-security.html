Web Security

Web security is highly essential as it improves your Brand reputation. Depending on the frequency and duration of the cyber-attacks, companies might loose in millions. Many a times, the company could have to pay if attacked due to ransomware. Your competitive edge, several years’ of revenue stream and growth potential could be heavily dependent on your IP and you certainly do not want to compromise on such a key asset. In many geographies, it has not only become mandatory to protect the data that you collect, even means of storing the data is highly regulated. Being prone to cyber-attacks will lead to being sued by customers, partners or both. Even if not regulated by government, companies would be typically upset if your PII (personally identifiable information) is exposed to cyber-criminals, which could be sold in the dark web. For all the above reasons and several of the unlisted ones, it is imperative for your business to guard against cyber-attacks.
